# Workforce Training Policy

> **Status:** Stub.

**Owner:** Founder (Security Officer)
**Implements:** 45 CFR §164.308(a)(5)
**Last reviewed:** [DATE]

## Scope

Every workforce member who has access to PHI completes training:
1. Before initial access
2. Annually thereafter
3. When material changes to policies or systems occur

For alpha, "workforce" is the founder. As the team grows, this policy
expands to cover contractors, advisors with access, and employees.

## Training topics

1. **HIPAA basics** — what PHI is, who is covered, what the rules
   require
2. **Cappy security policies** — `/compliance/SECURITY-RULES.md`
3. **Incident response** — how to identify, report, and respond to
   incidents
4. **Phishing and social engineering** — recognition and reporting
5. **Password and device security** — MFA, password manager use,
   device encryption, screen lock
6. **Access management** — least privilege, periodic review
7. **Working with PHI in dev** — never copying production data, using
   synthetic data only

## Evidence

Each training completion is logged with:
- Trainee name
- Training topics covered
- Date completed
- Materials used
- Signature or attestation

Training records retained for 6 years per §164.316(b)(2)(i).
