## Plain-language summary

Cappy helps families coordinate medication for their children. To do
that, we collect a small amount of information about your family and
the medications you log. We protect that information carefully, we do
not sell it, and you control it.

## What we collect

When you sign up:
- Your email address
- Optionally, your phone number
- A name you choose (other caregivers in your family see this)

When you set up your family:
- A name for your family (e.g., "The Smiths")
- For each child: a name you choose, their date of birth, and their
  weight as you record it

When you log medication:
- The time you logged the dose
- Which medication
- The amount
- Which child
- An optional note you write
- Which caregiver logged it

When you use NFC tags:
- The unique ID of each tag and which medication it is bound to in
  your family

Automatically:
- Your IP address (used briefly for security; we store only a hashed
  version)
- Diagnostic information from your device when something goes wrong
  (with personal information removed)

## What we do not collect

- We do not collect location data
- We do not collect contacts, photos, calendar, or other content from
  your device
- We do not use cookies or third-party trackers in the app
- We do not collect any information about people who are not in your
  family

## How we use it

We use this information to:
- Show you and your invited caregivers a shared record of doses
- Send the dose record to other caregivers' phones in real time
- Maintain an audit log so you can see who logged what and when
- Investigate and fix problems
- Comply with our legal obligations

We do not use your information to:
- Show you ads
- Sell to third parties
- Train AI models for any purpose unrelated to providing the service

## Who can see your information

- You and the caregivers you invite to your family
- Cappy's small team, only when troubleshooting at your request or as
  required for security
- Vendors that help us run the service, each of whom has signed a
  Business Associate Agreement (BAA) to protect your information

The vendors we currently use:
- Supabase (database, authentication, storage)
- Sentry (error tracking, with personal information removed)
- Postmark (transactional email)

We do not share your information with anyone else without your
permission, except as required by law.

## How we protect your information

- Sensitive fields are encrypted both in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest
  (AES-256 with envelope encryption)
- We maintain a tamper-evident audit log of every action
- Access to production data is limited and logged
- We follow the HIPAA Security Rule for technical, administrative, and
  physical safeguards

No system is perfectly secure. If we discover a breach affecting your
information, we will notify you within 60 days as required by HIPAA.

## Your rights

You can:
- Access the information we have about you
- Correct inaccurate information
- Delete your account and the data associated with it
- Export your data in a portable format
- Withdraw consent for any optional processing

To exercise these rights during alpha, email **privacy@cappy.app**.
We will respond within 30 days.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under
state law (California, Washington, others) or international law (GDPR
for European residents). We honor those rights. Contact us using the
email above.

## Children's information

Cappy collects information about children only as part of a family
record maintained by a parent or legal guardian. By creating a child
profile, you confirm that you are the parent, legal guardian, or
otherwise authorized to provide this information. We do not collect
information directly from children.

## Changes to this policy

If we materially change this policy, we will notify you in the app
and by email at least 30 days before the change takes effect.
