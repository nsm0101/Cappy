Weighing Tips

  • Use a calibrated scale for each measurement
  • Weigh at the same time of day
  • Remove shoes and heavy clothing
  • Record weight in kilograms for precision
  • Reassess weight every 2–4 weeks for children
💊

Dosing Intervals

  • Ibuprofen: every 6–8 hours (max 4 doses/day)
  • Acetaminophen: every 4–6 hours (max 5 doses/day)
  • Amoxicillin: every 8–12 hours
  • Azithromycin: once daily for 3–5 days
  • Never exceed the single-dose maximum
⚠️

Safety Reminders

  • Always verify doses with your prescriber
  • Check for allergies and drug interactions
  • Use weight-based dosing for children < 12 years
  • Log every dose with a timestamp in Cappy
  • Discard expired medications promptly
📏

Unit Conversions

  • 1 kg = 2.205 lbs
  • 1 mg/mL = 1 g/L
  • 1 teaspoon (tsp) = 5 mL
  • 1 tablespoon (tbsp) = 15 mL
  • 1 fluid ounce (fl oz) = 29.6 mL
